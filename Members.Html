Meet some of the key members responsible for making all of this that we have today possible! The key members in the Newark branch are the Chairmen (Mr Vito Scalletta), the Vice Chariman (Dani Rossi), and our newest executive teasurer (Pablo Paocilli). All of these men together make up the three heads of one of the more successful branches of the mafia. They've worked together to build and continue its success, from planning the events, making meaningful business connections, as well as finding new ways to give back to the community.

Mr Vito Scalletta is the oldest member to date at the branch in Newark. Mr Scalletta was born to a poor, Italian immigrant family in 1964. After living in poverty for many years, he decided to join the United States Army in his early teenage years to both provide for his family and serve for his country in 1983. He went on to serve for a total of 5 years, until he was forced to leave due to an injury. However, before he left he picked up multiple honors and medals, including the NAM and the National Defense Ribbon.
