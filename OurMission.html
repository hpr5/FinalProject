Our general mission is to give back to the community! Thats right, we are criminals gone right! We want to aid the people in need, and we need your help! All these fundraisers, all these events, all these connections! They are all meant for one purpose and one purpose only: To feed those in need. Every week we send out missions of our members to go to nearb homeless shelters or hotspots where it is known homeless people are, and we provide them with the aid that they need. Most of our aid comes form our proud sponsors that we team up with. By advertising their products and making their messages heard, we're able to spread more resources accross a larger population. Our company has been growing from the very beginning and we're only getting started.
